Furthermore, nearly 1 in 3 children under five are suffering from malnutrition n while 93% of children between 6 months and 2 years of age are not consuming the minimum acceptable diet. The system we are developing will help solve this issue and it is a web based expert system which diagnoses three childrenâ€™s diseases (kwashiorkor, marasmus and marasmus-kwashiorkor) caused by severe malnutrition. It works best for children under the age of 6. The system is mainly meant for use by community health workers but parents, guardians as well as school teachers will be able to check using the expert system if the child has a disease caused by malnutrition and also provide nutritional solutions to the diseases. This is important as there is a shortage of doctors and nurses in the country
