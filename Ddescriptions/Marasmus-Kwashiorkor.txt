Marasmic-Kwashiorkor is  is the third form of protein-energy malnutrion that combines features and symptoms of both marasmus and kwashiorkor.

Protein malnutritrion, or kwashiorkor or edematous malnutrition is mostly found in people living in geographical areas that have limited food resources.

A person with Marasmic-Kwashioker may be - extremely thin - show signs of wasting in areas of the body - have excessive fluid buildup in other parts
